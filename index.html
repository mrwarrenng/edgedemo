Why now

Medford knows its own market.

CNBC ranked Oregon 39th overall and 47th for business friendliness in 2025. Those statewide measures do not always reflect the realities of markets like Medford, where responsiveness, practical navigation, and local fit matter more than broad averages.

The opportunity

A service gap. A practical response.

After the SOU SBDC closure at the end of 2025, entrepreneurs and businesses still need guidance. EDGE extends the reach of the Economic Development Department without multiplying headcount.

What EDGE delivers

One platform. Six tools.

🧠

MEDA

AI business assistant for permits, zoning, programs, contacts, and next steps.

💡

Incentive Finder

Eight quick questions surface the right verified programs for the user.

📈

Indicators Dashboard

Medford economic data from 13+ federal and state sources, explained in plain language.

📋

Launch Wizard

Step-by-step roadmap from formation to first hire, with links, costs, and funding resources.

🔢

Cost Calculator

Helps businesses avoid surprise project costs and shows how Medford policymakers have worked to keep project execution clear and business-friendly.

🗺️

Area Intelligence Reports

Creates downloadable PDF reports with workforce, demographics, costs, and incentives tailored to a real project.
